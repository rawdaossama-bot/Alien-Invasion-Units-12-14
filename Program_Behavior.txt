1. Game Start
- The game window opens with a starry background.
- The spaceship appears at the bottom center of the screen.
- Background music or sound effects are not included at start.

2. Controls
- Move ship left: Left Arrow
- Move ship right: Right Arrow
- Fire bullets: Spacebar
- Quit game: Q

3. Ship Behavior
- Ship moves left/right with the specified speed.
- Ship cannot move beyond the left or right edge of the screen.

4. Bullet Behavior
- Maximum 5 bullets can exist on the screen at any given time.
- Bullets move upwards when fired.
- Each bullet firing plays a laser sound effect.

5. Manual Checks
- Verify ship moves smoothly left and right.
- Verify ship stops at screen edges.
- Fire bullets repeatedly and ensure only 5 bullets are on screen simultaneously.
- Check that laser sound plays every time a bullet is fired.
- Quit the game using Q or the window close button.

6. Notes
- All assets are in the `Assets` folder.
- Game speed and bullet limits are controlled in `Settings.py`.
- Any modifications to ship speed or bullet limits should be tested manually.
